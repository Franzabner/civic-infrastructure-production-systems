# Public-Safe QA Checklist

Status: scaffolded

- Confirm assumptions are synthetic.
- Confirm no donor data, student data, volunteer data, customer data, or Foundation-private data.
- Confirm no production CAD, production schematics, BOMs, Gerbers, or exact dimensions.
- Confirm no school deployment, production recycling line, live civic node, disaster-response deployment, manufacturing readiness, or certified safety claim.
- Confirm validation and review notes are present.
