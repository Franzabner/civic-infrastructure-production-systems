## Problem Statement

The context is a generic civic infrastructure production study surface. It may describe categories such as recycling automation studies, 3D-print workflow notes, QA assumptions, and material-flow review, but it must not describe a school deployment, production recycling line, live civic node deployment, disaster-response deployment, or manufacturing readiness.

## Foundation-Adjacent Boundary

This artifact may reference civic infrastructure as a domain. It does not claim Foundation authority, Foundation operations, donor data, student data, volunteer data, or Foundation-private data.

## Personal Proof-Surface Boundary

The artifact documents personal engineering reasoning and public-safe review discipline only.
