# AGENTS.md

This repo is a Phase 5 public scaffold for public-safe civic infrastructure production studies.

## Operating Rules

- Keep all content synthetic, public-safe, and reviewable.
- Treat the repo as Franzabner personal engineering proof. It does not speak as the Foundation.
- Do not imply operational Foundation programs, school deployment, production recycling line operation, live civic node deployment, disaster-response deployment, manufacturing readiness, certified safety, donor data, student data, volunteer data, Foundation-private data, customer data, private sites, private facility layouts, production CAD, production schematics, BOMs, Gerbers, exact dimensions, live operations, internal company product names, sealed designs, or sealed geometry.
- Keep status language limited to `planned`, `scaffolded`, `public scaffold`, `published scaffold`, and `not released` until human review approves any later release.
- Do not add remotes, push, publish, change metadata, or route this repo through `Franzabner` or `franzabner-proof-stack`.

## Done Means

Work is complete only when the requested files are present, civic authority boundaries are explicit, public/private/sealed boundaries are documented, validation passes, and review notes identify held items.
